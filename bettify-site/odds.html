How to read odds

  1. Decimal odds are multiplier values; multiply your stake by the decimal to see gross payout.
  2. The implied probability is 1 / decimal; Bettify displays both for clarity.
  3. Market lines (handicaps) reduce variance and can provide better edge for strategic bettors.
